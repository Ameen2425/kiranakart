About KiranaKart

A New Standard for Grocery Delivery

Founded with a simple obsession — freshness that never waits — KiranaKart connects local dark-stores, verified suppliers and a trained delivery fleet into one seamless experience. From the first tap to the knock on your door, every step is designed around speed, quality and trust. Today, KiranaKart serves millions of households across the country, delivering farm-fresh produce, dairy and daily essentials in as little as ten minutes.
